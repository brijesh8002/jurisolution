Trademark Registration

A Trademark is a distinctive sign, symbol, logo, word, phrase, design, or combination that identifies and distinguishes goods or services of one business from another.

  • Brand names
  • Company names
  • Logos
  • Taglines
  • Product names
  • Symbols / identifiers

Governed by the Trade Marks Act, 1999. Valid for 10 years and renewable indefinitely. The process generally takes 8 to 18 months. You can start using ™ immediately after filing.

Copyright Registration

Copyright protects original literary, artistic, musical, dramatic, and creative works.

  • Books and articles
  • Software and computer programs
  • Website content
  • Architectural works
  • Photographs
  • Paintings and artistic works
  • Films and videos
  • Music compositions

Copyright protection in India is governed by the Copyright Act, 1957..Although copyright exists automatically once the work is created, registration provides stronger legal evidence of ownership in case of disputes or infringement. The registration process generally takes 2 to 4 months, subject to objections or discrepancies during examination.
